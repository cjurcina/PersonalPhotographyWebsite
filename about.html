About Me & Darth Deuces Photography

Welcome to Darth Deuces Photography! My name is Chris AKA Darth Deuces, as I'm known on other media websites. I'm a big geek, and a fan of multiple different franchises. I'm also a collector of mainly action figures. About two years ago I discovered the art of toy photography on Instagram. since then, I've been an active toy photographer, expressing my love for these franchises and toys through my photographs. I'm mainly a fan of Star Wars, Marvel, Transformers, and DC, but I also really like franchises such as Halo and Spawn. This website is to give myself a personal space in which to share my work, that is connected yet seperate to my Instagram page. I also have prints avaiable for purchase, as well as commission services available. More information on that can be found here. I love to take toy photos and I love to share my photos with the collector and geek community. I still have much to learn, and I always welcome feedback. Thanks for stopping by, and most importantly, enjoy!
